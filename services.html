Social Engineering (White Hat)

White Hat social engineering is a legitimate and lawful method of penetration testing and security assessments. It involves creating false identities, scenarios, or situations in order to trick individuals into providing sensitive information or access to a system or network. It's also important to keep in mind that there are many different types of social engineering attacks, including phishing, vishing (voice over IP), smishing (SMS), whaling (targeting high-level executives), and spear phishing, pretexting, among others. When you're using white hat social engineering, it's important to follow the laws and regulations that apply in your area.
