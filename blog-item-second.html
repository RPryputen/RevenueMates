The main principles of building a business based on the SaaS model

SaaS is an abbreviation of the English words "Software as a service". These words are the essence of this business. Valuable data is located in the data center, and users have access to the data on the basis of a paid subscription. Often the saas business model uses cloud technology. The main goals of SaaS implementation are to reduce the user's costs and expand their capabilities. Subscription-based software can be run both through a web interface and on a mobile device. Also, this approach solves the problem of software updates, they are performed automatically by the SaaS provider. In addition, there are such services as Platform as a Service (PaaS) and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS). The approach is similar - the provider provides a paid subscription, which replaces a significant one-time investment.

What to consider before building a SaaS project

Before you launch a SaaS product you should decide what user data will be collected and stored in the cloud. Legislation in many countries is very sensitive to how SaaS startups handle user data. And failure to comply with legal requirements can lead to significant fines. SaaS business models should include different types of user subscriptions. It is desirable that among them there are various free test services for a limited period or with limited functionality. In this case, for the user of a SaaS based company it is vital to be sure that all his technical problems are solved and in case of difficulties he will get help from the support. It is quite possible that the user will need initial help in installing, configuring and mastering the functionality of the subscription provided. In addition to the fact that the saas product should solve the real problems of the customer, no less important is the marketing strategy to promote the finished saas product on the market. It should take into account the feedback received from the first users of the product. Often, the SaaS idea cannot be fully implemented because updates are slow to be made to the system. However, one has to put up with this peculiarity of SaaS based companies for the sake of user data security.

What are the recent SaaS ideas

If you are just thinking about starting a SaaS company, start by looking at other successful businesses in this area. To give an example of a successful SaaS startup, you need to look at the areas in which they are most active right now. The following areas of business have a good chance of success:

  • Social media engagement platforms
  • Platforms for content management.
  • A variety of services for freelancers.
  • Productivity tracking for remote employees.
  • Tools for working with rental properties.
  • Processing interviews from candidates for hiring.
  • Providing vendors with necessary payment solutions

To start a SaaS company, you don't have to start a big business right away. A Micro-SaaS based company can work in a very narrow niche such as email marketing optimization, keyword analysis for SEO, etc. So if you have a good SaaS idea, that's already a great reason to start a SaaS company. SaaS product development is a difficult task, and even the best SaaS idea will take a lot of time and effort to implement.

The newest trends in SaaS development

In today's world increasingly used neural network technology and AI. SaaS products also use modern technology. For example, there are quite a few startups that offer access to valuable data on a subscription basis. This can be data from chemistry, biology, biochemistry. AI here helps pick up the necessary formula or molecule, for which the SaaS product takes money from the customer. There are also SaaS products that allow farmers to get yield advice based on processing with AI sensors placed in their fields. It is becoming a good solution to use AI to visualize problems associated with the operation of various equipment, with the structural details of buildings. Already, SaaS solutions related to the processing of voice data received from customers are widely used. In the form of SaaS, vendors also get chatbots with complex user interaction logic. And there are SaaS projects that provide services related to Big Data, complex mathematics and cryptography. The world of SaaS is constantly evolving and expanding, and there is no doubt that the list of ideas and trends mentioned in this article is far from complete.

Major deals such as Uniphore ($400 million) and Chargebee ($250 million) raised not only the Indian SaaS market, but also energized the SaaS industry on a global scale.

We see SaaS technology successfully paving the way, changing the way we approach marketing, imaging, and collaboration on various projects. A SaaS startup with an interesting idea can count on venture capital support, which in some cases is measured in hundreds of millions of dollars.

The SaaS model is particularly interesting for young companies to test new ideas. They can get started without having to build distribution networks for their products or invest large sums in sales teams. Instead, they rely heavily on online advertising and viral marketing. However, the cost of attracting new customers initially exceeds the revenue generated by subscription fees. If the business model is viable, the way out is to raise venture capital. This explains the reason why the SaaS industry and venture capital are so well matched.
